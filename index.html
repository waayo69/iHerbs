Welcome To iHerbs

iHerbs is a family run wildcrafting, manufacturing, online store, training and consulting business established in 2010. We are located 30 kilometers from the city of Yellowknife, Northwest Territories.

We operate our facility 100% off-grid in the sub-arctic wilderness using solar energy. The products we create are carefully crafted in small batches, using hand-foraged wild herbs, berries, lichens and fungi.
